Abdominal Muscles

Crunches:
            Lie on your back with knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Place your hands behind your head and lift your upper body off the ground,engaging your abdominal muscles.


 

Russian Twists:
            Sit on the floor with your knees bent and feet lifted. Lean back slightly, forming a V-shape with your body. Hold a weight (e.g., medicine ball, plate) and rotate your torso from side to side.


Leg Raises:
             Lie on your back with your hands by your sides. Lift your legs straight up towards the ceiling, then slowly lower them back down without touching the floor.


Plank:
            Get into a push-up position with your forearms on the ground. Keep your body in a straight line from your head to your heels. Hold for as long as you can.
 

Back Muscles

Seated cable row
             Top left: This exercise involves pulling a cable handle towards your chest while seated on a machine. It primarily works the latissimus dorsi (lats), rhomboids, and trapezius muscles.


Bent-over barbell row:
             Top right:This exercise involves bending over at the hips and pulling a barbell towards your chest. It also works the lats, rhomboids, and trapezius, but with a greater emphasis on the lower back muscles.


One-arm cable row:
             Bottom left: This exercise involves pulling a cable handle towards your chest with one arm while seated on a machine. It is a good exercise for isolating the lats on each side.


Face pull:
             Bottom right: This exercise involves pulling a cable handle towards your face at a diagonal angle. It primarily works the rhomboids and trapezius muscles, and can also help improve posture.


Biceps Muscles

Bicep Curls: Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing forward. Bend your elbows and curl the weights up towards your shoulders. Hammer Curls: Hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing inward. Bend your elbows and curl the weights up towards your shoulders. Concentration Curls: Sit on a bench and hold a dumbbell in one hand. Rest your elbow on your knee and curl the weight up towards your shoulder.

Calves

Calf Raises: Stand tall with your feet shoulder-width apart. Raise your heels off the ground, then lower them back down.

Donkey Calf Raises: Get on all fours and place a weight on your back. Raise your heels off the ground, then lower them back down.

Seated Calf Raises: Sit on a calf raise machine and place your feet on the footplate. Raise your heels off the ground, then lower them back down

Chest Muscles

Push-ups: Get into a push-up position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body towards the ground, then push yourself back up.

Bench Press: Lie on a bench and hold a barbell with an overhand grip. Press the barbell up towards the ceiling, then lower it back down to your chest.

Incline Dumbbell Press: Lie on an incline bench and hold a dumbbell in each hand. Press the dumbbells up towards the ceiling, then lower them back down to your chest.

Dumbbell Flyes: Lie on a bench and hold a dumbbell in each hand with your palms facing each other. Bring your arms out to the sides, then bring them back together.

forearms

Wrist Curls: Sit at a table and hold a weight in your hand. Rest your forearm on the table and curl your wrist up.

Reverse Wrist Curls: Sit at a table and hold a weight in your hand with your palm facing down. Rest your forearm on the table and curl your wrist up.

Farmer's Walks: Hold a heavy weight in each hand and walk around.

Glutes

Hamstring Curls: Lie on your stomach and bend your knees. Curl your heels up towards your glutes.

Good Mornings: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell behind your neck. Bend forward at the waist, keeping your back straight.

Leg Curls: Sit on a leg curl machine and curl the weight up towards your glutes.

Hamstring Curls: Lie on your stomach and bend your knees. Curl your heels up towards your glutes.

Good Mornings: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell behind your neck. Bend forward at the waist, keeping your back straight.

Leg Curls: Sit on a leg curl machine and curl the weight up towards your glutes.
